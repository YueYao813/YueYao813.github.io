About

I am a PhD candidate working on trajectory prediction, multi-agent interaction, and robot motion planning for autonomous driving. My work has appeared in RA-L, IROS, and ITSC. I build learning-based forecasting models that are interpretable and deployable.

Research Interests

  • Interaction-aware planning in multi-agent systems

  • Closed-loop evaluation & sim-to-real
